What We Offer

BMI calculator: This innovative tool allows you to quicly calculate your BMI by just asking your hight in feets and your weight in pounds. not only this but it also allows you to then view your calulated BMI on a grapgh so you know if you are underweight, healthy or overweight.

Stress checker: Our stress assesment tool is yet another innovative tool which allows indivduals to monitor there stress level and therefore preserve their psychological well-being by merely asking them 10 simple questions and taking 5 minutes of there time. This tool is based on Perceived Stress Scale and hence it is extremely accurate.

Age predictor: Wondering how your life decsions affect your age ? Our age predictor is there to help. it asks you a number of questions about your habbits and life decsions and tries to predict your average age based on your answer.

Grocery Getter: Wondering how much calories your evening snack contains !!!, we got your back with grocerygetter. A toool that can help you track your calories by providing you the information about the food you are about to consume.
